## 🫂 Human Safety & Social Navigation Survey
The safety reading guide in this repository is centered on **human-robot safety in shared spaces**: **socially compliant navigation**, **human-aware motion**, **crowd interaction**, **personal-space preservation**, and **preference-aware behavior**.

For a dedicated reading guide, use [Safety_nav_list.md](Safety_nav_list.md). That page focuses on:
- social navigation surveys and benchmarks,
- human-aware and socially compliant navigation methods,
- human preference, dialogue, and interaction-aware planning,
- broader safety, robustness, and attack-related papers that are relevant to safe embodied navigation.
